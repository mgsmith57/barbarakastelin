WHEN SNOW FELL

The White Russian Brodskys, who have lost their glamorous aristocratic life in St. Petersburg, love, lie and fight in their Oxfordshire Mansion.

Money runs out. Countess Olga puts their sacred Ikon up for sale and asks the Soviet Union for compensation.

Which brings Comrade Voronov into their life and turns it into a nightmare.

Is it not said that the Russian soul is patriotism, passion and vanity? Does the glamour of Tsarist aristocracy not still appeal?

It is now 100 years since the Russian Revolution wiped out that glamour. It happened to my family.

THE PARROT TREE

A lost key in a suit pocket opens many doors for Vivien when she decided to find its owner…

The planet’s ancient forest has powers over mankind still. To preserve it is the dying wish of the Baron von Keyserling. Filming in the jungle encounters obstacles, greater even than the model Leandra and her tantrums.

A love affair blossoms amidst parrots, butterflies and passion flowers.

But deep secrets rise to the surface at the death of the Baron. Who will inherit the estate? What tortured road led him from war-torn Czechoslovakia to fame? And how does it shape Vivien’s destiny?
