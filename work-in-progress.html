Work in Progress

My next novel will be about a woman selling flowers in London.

As this is not just shaken out of my sleeve, and I need to acquaint myself with the heroine in my imagination, and find out who she will meet, I am concurrently working on a collection of short stories, just to keep writing being written.

These short stories play in different parts of the world and, only too often, reveal the darker side of human nature.
